About Hadji Soab Olawale Iron Rods

Established in 2005, Hadji Soab Olawale Iron Rods has grown to become a leading supplier of premium quality iron rods in Nigeria. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for contractors and builders nationwide.

We source our materials from the most reputable manufacturers and subject them to rigorous quality control processes to ensure they meet international standards.

Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to providing exceptional service, timely delivery, and expert advice to help you choose the right products for your construction needs.
